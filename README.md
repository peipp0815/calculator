# calculator

A small calculator with full keyboard support.

Explanation of calculator buttons and their expressions on the keyboard:

"AC": All Clear; wipes all data; Escape-button on your keyboard

"DEL": Delete; delete last entered digit; Backspace-button on your keyboard

"=": get result; press either "Enter" or "=" on your keyboard

"+": addition

"-": subtraction

"\*": multiplication

"/": division
